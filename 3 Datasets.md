## Dataset 2: Restaurant Sales Dataset
##### Description: 
This dataset captures sales transactions from Balaji Restaurant, a local Indian eatery. It includes variables such as order ID, date, item name and type (Fast-food or Beverages), item price, quantity, total transaction amount, payment method, staff gender, and time of sale (Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Night, or Midnight). The dataset provides detailed insights into daily operations, customer preferences, and sales patterns in a loacal indian resturants.
##### Reason for choosing: 
I chose this dataset because it offers real-world business data that can be used to explore sales performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency. It also come up with very interesting analyses such as understanding revenue trends and time of day on sales.
##### Source: 
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rajatsurana979/fast-food-sales-report
